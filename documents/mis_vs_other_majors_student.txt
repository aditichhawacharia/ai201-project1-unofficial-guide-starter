Currently an MIS senior. The major is pretty straightforward most of the time. The only issue would be if you have zero coding experience, or if you don't like project work. There are a few classes within the major that are definitely a bit difficult/time consuming — 333K and 374. It's a really interesting major though! I wouldn't recommend it if you're strictly looking at GPA. Maybe look into Management, SCM, or International Business?
